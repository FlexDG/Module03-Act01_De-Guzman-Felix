Your 2021 U-Week LoL Champions!

In this blogsite, you will get to know how the LoL team of CCS managed to win the championship during the 2021 University Week, undefeated. You will also get to know the skilled players which made such an achievement possible, punching through top teams in the university such CAMP and CBA who are known to be powerhouses when it comes to esports and gaming in general.

Trivia ~
Did you know, Lanz, the marksman of the team has been consistently making it to the top 100 leaderboard in the PH server for the past 3 seasons?

ABOUT US

Jungler

Felix De Guzman (Team Captain)

The captain of the CCS-LoL Team who lead the cavalry into winning the bread , Felix, is a solid jungler who kept good pace with the best junglers that the tournament had to offer.

ADC

Lanz Dagdag
(MVP)

Lanz, our marksman, won the most valuable player award for the whole tournament, and for a reason. He outskilled every single one of his opponents and displayed true gaming sense and finesse all throughout the whole competition.

mid

Kenan Punsalang
(ACE)

Kenan, our midlaner, was the player who didn't really need any help from the team, he knew how to carry his own in all of our games and he also added that "carry factor" which was a big part in making us win the whole thing.

Top

Jello Mangune (Surprise Factor)

Jello, our toplaner, was came big in clutch situations which contributed a ton in leaving the tournament undeafeted.
